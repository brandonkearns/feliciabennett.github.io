Jack Daniel’s Honey Block Party

The Jack Daniel’s Block Party was the first event of the Neighborhood Flavor Series in Atlanta. The july day party was centered around food, drinks, community, and music. As Production Manager for St. Beauty, Felicia managed all technical sound needs for their headlining performance. Lighting queues, visual assets, sound equipment, backline, and stage setup were all her responsibility to ensure the success of their show.

Dirty Computer Tour w/ Janelle Monae & St. Beauty

Janelle Monae’s 28-date Dirty Computer Tour kicked off in Seattle on June 11, 2018. Felicia was Tour Manager for St. Beauty, the opening act for Janelle every night of tour. This two and a half month North American and Canadian run took them cross country. Her duties included covering all routing and transportation from city to city, hospitality, lodging for band and crew, running the day of show itinerary, and ensuring all production needs were met. As lead she must keep everything running smoothly and look after all finances.

Wisdom Is Power Tour

This was her first ever tour and role as a full time manager. This was a 3 week, 15 date tour that took her to all 3 coasts. Deante Hitchcock is a prolific Atlanta rapper that Rapsody invited to be one of her opening acts. Starting November 29th, Felicia drove Deante’s band and crew to NYC for the first east coast show of tour at S.O.B.’s. It was sold out with a line out the door. Fans were craning their necks hoping to catch a glimpse of the Deante, Rapsody, and Ninth Wonder before the show. This winter tour was 90% sold out before the tour even started. The art of lyricism and real hip-hop was showcased on this tour. Felicia managed to successfully get Deante’s team to all venues and ended every night with a great show and new fans. Laila’s Wisdom and So Much For Good Luck are albums you need to put your ears on.

A3C Festival 2018

The 14th annual A3C festival was a celebration of hip-hop that had a lineup that consisted of performers Wu-Tang Clan, Lil Wayne, J.I.D, Deante Hitchcock, and much more. Deante Hitchcock performed on the main stage and Felicia performed duties as his Tour Manager. He had a 30 min slot with his DJ. As Tour Manager she handled all advance correspondence with the A3C Stage Manager, Artist Liaison, Program Director, Deante, his manager, and all crew leading up to the festival. In addition, she handled the run of show, all audio/visual requirements, technical specs, scheduling, equipment inventory, credentials, hospitality, and communication on site at this outdoor festival.

@feliciadoee

Felicia Bennett is a marketing professional, tour manager, and artist consultant. She is passionate about promoting and fostering the live music scene. An Atlanta native, she has been immersed in music and the culture her whole life. As a result of her work at Sony ATV in NYC and Sarm Studios in London, she has taken her talents back to ATL at her new home, Live Nation.

She has marketed over 150 shows in the Atlanta market for the Live Nation Clubs and Theaters division. She’s toured with artists Janelle Monae as St. Beauty’s tour manager on the Dirty Computer tour and with Rapsody on the Laila’s Wisdom tour as Deante Hitchcock’s tour manager. Her work has taken her all over the US and Canada with her eyes set on international endeavors. From Coachella to Rolling Loud to her own consulting and events company Get A Room, she’s always looking for new creative ways to support artists.
